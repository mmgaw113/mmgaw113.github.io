Texas Throw 'Em

Texas Throw 'Em

Texas Throw 'Em was my Graduate Capstone project at RIT. You play as a Stage Magician antihero that uses their love and skill of throwing cards to commit crimes all throughout the wild west. Throughout this journey, players will rack up a hefty bounty on their head, with their end goal to escape the town of Gunflats before the law catches up to them. Don't get caught or you'll lose a hefty chuck of everything you collected thus far! Download and Play here!
