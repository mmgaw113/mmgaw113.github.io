Finger Guns

Texas Throw 'Em

Finger Guns is a Narrative Drive Virtual Reality shooter where you play as Ajax, a tech savy scientist tasked with creating advanced weaponry. This prompts you to create, The Finger Guns! After whitnessing the true power of this technology, your boss goes on a killing rampage and takes the finger guns for himself, leaving you defenseless. Your task is to recover the Finger Guns and escape with your life!

This project was worked on during two seperate Semesters of Graduate School at RIT. After the initial semester of work and a post mortem for the project, we decided that although Unity is a great game engine, we wanted a fresh start. So the project was restarted and continued in Unreal Engine the following year. Fundementally, the main mechanics for the game stayed the same with the only difference being visuals.
