ArcGIS Maps SDK Samples for Unity

Unity Sample Project

The ArcGISMaps SDK for Game Engines is a tool that allows people to bring GIS data into game engines and visualize it using the rendering capabilities of the engine. Since I joined the team in 2022, I have worked on a multitude of samples to demonstrate the capabilities of the SDK. The XR Table Top Sample is one of the samples I created. This sample allows users to visualize there data on a virtual table top in either Virtual Reality or Mixed Reality. This sample utilizes Open XR and Hand Tracking (Meta Quest only), as well as all desktop XR features provided by Unity and Open XR. Please feel free to take a look at the Public Samples Repoository, as this is just one of many samples I have worked on. Check it out here!
